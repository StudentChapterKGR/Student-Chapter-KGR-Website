What is IUCEE

IUCEE, which has its headquarters at the University of Massachusetts Lowell, was founded by a group of academic and industrial specialists from India and the United States, and has now expanded to include professionals from other nations.

IUCEE's mission is to increase the quality and global relevance of Indian engineering education. IUCEE acknowledged that maintaining its purpose needed a transformational ecosystem that focuses on faculty growth, student development, curriculum improvement, improved instructional tools, and engineering education research.

About us

KG Reddy College of
Engineering and Technology

The college has been established by Aristotle Educational Society which was started in the year 2000 by young, highly qualified and motivated group of individuals with an intention to provide high quality technical education to students from rural and low socio-economic communities.

Since its inception, the society has established multiple and diverse institutions in the higher education space with a goal to graduate professionals who are trained and prepared to become leaders in their respective fields.
